Studio Rules
& Policies

Please read through our policies carefully before making a booking. They exist to ensure every client has a smooth, fair, and enjoyable experience at HRM Aesthetic Haven.

By completing your booking and payment, you confirm that you have read and agreed to all studio rules and policies listed on this page.

01

Booking Policy

We operate strictly by appointment — no walk-ins are accepted under any circumstance.

All prices are final and non-negotiable.

Your booking is only confirmed after full payment has been received.

Payments cover space rental only and do not include photography or editing fees.

Be specific with the time you book — your session starts exactly as scheduled, not when you arrive.

The minimum booking duration is 1 hour. We do not accept 30-minute bookings.

We strongly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

02

Lateness & Arrival

Arrive at the exact time you booked. Do not bring guests who have not been included in your payment.

A 10-minute grace period is allowed. After that, the time is deducted from your session.

Failure to arrive or reschedule results in a forfeited session with no refund.

After 10 min late Time lost Deducted from your session, no extension given
30 min late ₦5,000 Lateness fee charged or time deducted
Mid-session extension ₦10,000 Subject to studio availability only
03

Rescheduling Policy

1 free reschedule is allowed if requested at least 3 days before your original booking.

Rescheduling less than 2 days before your session incurs a ₦5,000 fee.

Same-day changes incur a ₦10,000 fee.

All reschedules are subject to studio availability and are not guaranteed.

04

Studio Conduct & Damages

Food, drinks, eating, and smoking are strictly prohibited inside the studio at all times.

Makeup must be completed before arriving. Doing makeup inside the studio is not permitted.

Please take all personal belongings when you leave. We are not responsible for lost or forgotten items.

Do not move chairs or studio equipment without permission from management.

Any damage caused to the studio, props, or equipment during your session will be charged to the client.

05

Cancellation & Refunds

Refunds are processed within 3 working days of a valid cancellation request.

Same-day cancellations and no-shows receive no refund.

More than 3 days before 75% Refund issued within 3 working days
Within 3 days 50% Partial refund processed within 3 working days
Same-day or no-show 0% No refund issued for same-day cancellations
06

Extension Policy

Extensions are only available subject to studio availability at the time of your session.

If another booking is scheduled immediately after your session, an extension will not be permitted.

Extension requests must be made before the end of your session and approved by management.

Transparency

Why We Have These Fees

We understand fees can feel frustrating, but they are essential to keeping the studio running fairly for every client. Here is why they exist.

Once a booking is paid for, that time slot is locked — no other client can book it, regardless of demand.

Late arrivals and last-minute rescheduling cause direct financial loss that cannot be recovered.

Fees ensure every client is treated equally and the studio operates without disruption.

We always aim to be reasonable. If you have an emergency, reach out — we handle situations on a case-by-case basis.
