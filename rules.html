Studio Rules & Policies

  • We operate strictly by bookings only — no walk-ins allowed.
  • All prices are final and cannot be negotiated.
  • Your booking is confirmed only after full payment has been made.
  • Payments cover space rental only and do not include photography or editing fees.
  • Be clear and specific with the time you book; your reserved time starts exactly as scheduled, not when you arrive.
  • The minimum booking time is 1 hour; we do not accept 30-minute bookings.
  • We strongly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.
  • Please be on time for your booking. your time begins at the exact hour you reserved.
  • Clients must arrive at the exact time they booked and should not bring extra guests not included in their payment.
  • A 10-minute grace period is allowed for late arrival.
  • After 10 minutes, the extra time is deducted from your session.
  • ₦5,000 lateness fee if you're up to 30 mins late (or time is deducted).
  • Extension (₦10,000) is allowed if the session ends and you’re already in the studio—subject to availability.
  • Failure to arrive or reschedule = forfeited session. No refunds.
  • 1 free reschedule if done at least 3 days before the original booking.
  • ₦5,000 fee if rescheduled less than 2 days before.
  • ₦10,000 same-day change fee.
  • All reschedules depend on studio availability.
  • Food, drinks, eating, and smoking are strictly not allowed in the studio.
  • Makeup must be done before arriving; doing makeup inside the studio is not permitted.
  • Please take all your personal items with you — we are not responsible for lost or forgotten items.
  • Do not move chairs or studio equipment unless permitted by management.
  • Cancel more than 3 days before booking: 75% refund.
  • Cancel within 3 days: 50% refund.
  • Same-day cancellations or no-shows: no refund.
  • Refunds are processed within 3 workingdays.
  • Extensions depend on availability.
  • If there's a booking right after, an extension won't be allowed.
  • Bookings are reserved exclusively once paid for.
  • No other clients are accepted for that slot.
  • Late arrivals or rescheduling cause financial loss.
  • Fees help us manage operations and avoid disruptions.
